Linking to Splitwise is only used by the app for
  1. Obtaining your Splitwise ID
  2. Adding the app's admin user as your Splitwise friend, so you can be added to Splitwise groups.

Your Splitwise authentication credentials are discarded after performing the above actions. No other actions are performed on your behalf by the RSVP app.

But, you are encouraged to revoke permissions for the app to your Splitwise account once these actions have been performed. {% if event %} You can find this link on your RSVP app profile, later. {% endif %}

The authorisation page shows that the RSVP app is requesting a whole bunch of permissions since the Splitwise API is not granular enough to give permissions only for obtaining user information, and/or adding friends.

If you are still skeptical, you can contact the app admins to manually peform the two steps listed above.
