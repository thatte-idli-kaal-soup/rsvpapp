Organize and manage team events easily!

Features

Simple RSVPs

  • Call-in for an event simply by clicking on a link
  • Easily manage RSVPs for multiple events concurrently
  • Keep WhatsApp groups still usable for actual discussions and chat
  • Easily see gender ratio for a practice
  • Keep track of player attendance

Player Information Management

  • Single DB with all player information, past and present
  • Easily search for a player's information
  • Quickly share player information for an event. Useful for sharing info with venue management, insurance agents, etc.
  • Create Splitwise groups from events with a single click

Organize Team Media

  • Automatically shared Google Drive among all players
  • Share photos to Google Drive with as much ease as sharing on WhatsApp
  • Don't lose valuable videos and pictures in WhatsApp groups, etc.
